# Example fiction manuscript for audiobook generation

# Chapter 1: The Arrival
Alice stepped off the train, her heart pounding. "I can't believe I'm finally here," she whispered.

Bob, waiting on the platform, waved. "Welcome to New Town, Alice!" he called.

# Chapter 2: The Mystery
The next morning, Alice found a strange note on her door. "Meet me at midnight," it read.

"Who could have left this?" Alice wondered aloud.

Bob shrugged. "Maybe it's just a prank."
