# GOAT Content Ownership Agreement

## Ownership Terms for AI-Enhanced Content Creation

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

This Content Ownership Agreement ("Agreement") governs the ownership and usage rights for content created, processed, or enhanced through GOAT's AI-powered content preparation services. GOAT is a **preparation service only** - we help you create professional content using AI tools, but you retain complete ownership of all content.

---

## 2. Definitions

### Key Terms
- **"User Content"**: Any content, text, images, audio, or data you upload or provide to GOAT
- **"AI-Enhanced Content"**: Content that has been processed, enhanced, or generated using GOAT's AI tools
- **"Output Content"**: The final deliverables produced by GOAT's services
- **"Preparation Services"**: GOAT's AI-powered content formatting, enhancement, and production services
- **"Commercial Use"**: Any use of content for business, marketing, or revenue-generating purposes

---

## 3. User Content Ownership

### Your Exclusive Ownership
- **Complete Ownership**: You retain 100% ownership of all User Content
- **No Transfer**: GOAT does not acquire any ownership rights to your User Content
- **No Licensing**: GOAT does not obtain any license to use your User Content
- **No Restrictions**: You can use your User Content however you wish

### What You Grant to GOAT
- **Processing License**: You grant GOAT a limited, revocable license to process your User Content solely for the purpose of providing Preparation Services
- **Technical Access**: You grant GOAT technical access to your User Content for the duration of processing
- **Storage License**: You grant GOAT permission to temporarily store your User Content during processing

### License Termination
- **Automatic Termination**: All licenses terminate automatically upon completion of services
- **Manual Termination**: You can revoke all licenses at any time
- **Data Deletion**: GOAT must delete all your content upon license termination
- **No Residual Rights**: GOAT retains no rights to your content after termination

---

## 4. AI-Enhanced Content Ownership

### Your Ownership of AI Output
- **Complete Ownership**: You own 100% of all AI-Enhanced Content and Output Content
- **No AI Rights**: GOAT does not retain any ownership rights to AI-generated content
- **No Derivative Claims**: GOAT does not claim rights to derivatives of AI-enhanced content
- **Commercial Freedom**: You can commercially use, license, or sell AI-enhanced content

### AI Training Data
- **No Training Usage**: GOAT does not use your content to train AI models
- **Anonymized Data Only**: GOAT may use anonymized, aggregated data for service improvement
- **No Content Reuse**: Your specific content is never reused for AI training
- **Data Separation**: User content is kept separate from AI training datasets

### Attribution Rights
- **No Attribution Required**: You are not required to attribute GOAT for AI-enhanced content
- **Optional Attribution**: You may voluntarily attribute GOAT if desired
- **Branding Rights**: You can rebrand AI-enhanced content as your own
- **Removal Rights**: You can remove any GOAT branding from deliverables

### Copyright Protection
- **Your Copyright**: You retain copyright to all original User Content
- **AI-Generated Copyright**: You own copyright to AI-generated enhancements
- **Combined Works**: You own copyright to combinations of your content and AI enhancements
- **International Protection**: Copyright protection applies globally

### Trademark Rights
- **Your Trademarks**: Your trademarks remain your exclusive property
- **No Dilution**: GOAT does not use your trademarks in any way
- **Protection**: GOAT respects and protects your trademark rights
- **Registration**: You maintain all trademark registrations and rights

### Patent Rights
- **Your Patents**: Any patented processes or inventions remain your property
- **No Claims**: GOAT does not make patent claims on AI-enhanced processes
- **Innovation Rights**: You retain rights to innovations created through our services
- **Protection**: GOAT does not challenge your patent rights

- **Licensing Rights**: You can license AI-enhanced content to third parties

- **No Exclusivity**: You are not restricted from using competitors' services

## 7. Data Usage and Privacy

### Service Data Usage
- **Processing Data**: GOAT uses your content only for the specific service requested
- **Temporary Storage**: Content is stored only during processing and deleted afterward
- **Access Controls**: Only authorized systems access your content during processing
- **Audit Trails**: All access to your content is logged and auditable

### Privacy Protections
- **Zero-Knowledge Processing**: GOAT cannot access the content of encrypted files
- **End-to-End Encryption**: All data transmission is encrypted
- **Secure Storage**: Content is stored with bank-level encryption
- **Access Logging**: All access attempts are logged and monitored

### Data Deletion
- **Automatic Deletion**: Content is automatically deleted after processing
- **Manual Deletion**: You can request immediate deletion at any time
- **Complete Removal**: Deleted content is permanently removed from all systems
- **No Backups**: Deleted content is not retained in backups
