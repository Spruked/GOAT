# GOAT Cookie Policy

## Cookie Usage and Privacy Notice

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how GOAT ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. This policy helps you understand how we use cookies and how you can control them.

---

## 2. What Are Cookies?

### Types of Cookies
- **Session Cookies**: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies**: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period
- **First-Party Cookies**: Cookies set directly by our website
- **Third-Party Cookies**: Cookies set by third-party services we use

### Other Technologies
- **Web Beacons**: Small graphic images that track website usage
- **Local Storage**: Browser storage for larger amounts of data
- **Session Storage**: Temporary browser storage for session data
- **Pixels**: Small images that track email opens and link clicks

## 3. Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Always Active)

#### Authentication Cookies
- **Purpose**: Keep you logged in and secure your account
- **Type**: Session cookies
- **Duration**: Session only
- **Data Collected**: Session tokens, user preferences

#### Security Cookies
- **Purpose**: Protect against fraud and security threats
- **Type**: Session and persistent cookies
- **Duration**: Up to 30 days
- **Data Collected**: Security tokens, risk indicators

#### Load Balancing Cookies

- **Type**: Session cookies

- **Data Collected**: Server routing information

- **Type**: Persistent cookies
- **Duration**: 1 year
- **Data Collected**: Language settings, UI preferences

#### Feature Cookies
- **Purpose**: Remember your choices for platform features
- **Type**: Persistent cookies
- **Duration**: 6 months
- **Data Collected**: Feature preferences, customization settings

### Analytics Cookies

#### Usage Analytics
- **Purpose**: Understand how users interact with our platform
- **Type**: Persistent cookies
- **Duration**: 2 years

#### Performance Monitoring
- **Purpose**: Monitor platform performance and identify issues
- **Type**: Session cookies
- **Duration**: Session only
- **Data Collected**: Error logs, performance metrics

### Marketing Cookies (Optional)

#### Campaign Tracking
- **Purpose**: Track effectiveness of marketing campaigns
- **Type**: Persistent cookies
- **Duration**: 1 year
- **Data Collected**: Campaign source, referral information
- **Activation**: Only active with user consent

## 5. Cookie Management

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser settings:

#### Chrome
- Click the three dots → Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- Block third-party cookies or all cookies

#### Firefox
- Click the menu button → Preferences → Privacy & Security
- Choose Custom settings for cookies

#### Safari
- Safari → Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- Block all cookies or manage individual sites

#### Edge
- Click the three dots → Settings → Cookies and site permissions
- Manage cookie settings

### Our Cookie Preferences
- **Cookie Banner**: Accept or reject non-essential cookies
- **Account Settings**: Manage cookie preferences in your account
- **Opt-out Links**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Do Not Track**: Respect Do Not Track browser signals
