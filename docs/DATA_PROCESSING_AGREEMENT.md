# GOAT Data Processing Agreement

## GDPR & CCPA Compliant Data Processing Terms

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") governs the processing of personal data by GOAT in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). GOAT acts as a data processor for user content and personal information.

---

## 2. Definitions

### Key Terms
- **"Personal Data"**: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **"Data Subject"**: The individual whose personal data is processed
- **"Data Controller"**: The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
- **"Data Processor"**: GOAT, which processes personal data on behalf of the Data Controller
- **"Processing"**: Any operation performed on personal data
- **"Sub-processor"**: Third parties engaged by GOAT to process personal data

---

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

### GOAT as Data Processor
- **Processing Authority**: GOAT processes personal data only as instructed by users (Data Controllers)
- **Purpose Limitation**: Personal data is processed only for the specific purposes agreed upon
- **Data Security**: GOAT implements appropriate technical and organizational measures
- **Compliance**: GOAT ensures compliance with applicable data protection laws

### User as Data Controller
- **Instruction Authority**: Users determine the purposes and means of processing
- **Consent Management**: Users are responsible for obtaining necessary consents
- **Data Subject Rights**: Users handle data subject requests and communications
- **Legal Compliance**: Users ensure their instructions comply with applicable laws

---

## 4. Data Processing Details

### Categories of Personal Data
- **Account Data**: Name, email address, account credentials
- **Content Data**: User-generated content that may contain personal data
- **Usage Data**: IP addresses, device information, usage patterns
- **Payment Data**: Billing information processed through third-party providers
- **Communication Data**: Support tickets, feedback, and correspondence

### Processing Purposes
- **Service Provision**: Processing necessary to provide content preparation services
- **Account Management**: User account creation, authentication, and management
- **Payment Processing**: Secure payment processing through authorized providers
- **Customer Support**: Providing technical support and service assistance
- **Security**: Protecting against fraud, abuse, and security threats

### Processing Activities
- **Collection**: Receiving personal data from users and their devices
- **Storage**: Secure storage of personal data in encrypted databases
- **Processing**: AI-powered content enhancement and formatting
- **Transmission**: Secure data transmission between systems and users
- **Deletion**: Secure deletion of personal data upon user request or account closure

---

## 5. Data Security Measures

### Technical Security
- **Encryption**: End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control with multi-factor authentication
- **Network Security**: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular security audits
- **Data Minimization**: Collection and processing limited to necessary data only

### Organizational Security
- **Staff Training**: Regular data protection training for all personnel
- **Access Logging**: Comprehensive logging of all data access and processing
- **Incident Response**: 24/7 incident response team for security breaches
- **Regular Audits**: Annual security audits and penetration testing

### Physical Security
- **Data Centers**: SOC 2 compliant data centers with physical security
- **Backup Security**: Encrypted backups with secure storage and transmission
- **Disaster Recovery**: Comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity plans
- **Destruction Procedures**: Secure destruction of physical media containing personal data

---

## 6. Data Subject Rights

### Access Rights
- **Data Access**: Data subjects can request access to their personal data
- **Processing Information**: Information about how their data is processed
- **Copy Provision**: Provision of copies of personal data in portable format
- **Processing Confirmation**: Confirmation of whether personal data is being processed

### Rectification Rights
- **Data Correction**: Right to have inaccurate personal data rectified
- **Incomplete Data**: Right to have incomplete personal data completed
- **Update Requests**: Ability to update personal data through account settings

### Erasure Rights ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Data Deletion**: Right to erasure of personal data in certain circumstances
- **Withdrawal Consent**: Right to withdraw consent and request data deletion
- **Objection Rights**: Right to object to processing in certain situations
- **Automated Processing**: Right to not be subject to automated decision-making

### Other Rights
- **Processing Restriction**: Right to restrict processing of personal data
- **Data Portability**: Right to receive personal data in structured format
- **Objection Rights**: Right to object to direct marketing and profiling
- **No Automated Decisions**: Protection against automated decision-making without consent

---

## 7. Data Retention

### Retention Periods
- **Account Data**: Retained for the duration of the account plus 3 years
- **Content Data**: Retained only during active processing, deleted upon completion
- **Usage Data**: Retained for 2 years for security and analytics purposes
- **Payment Data**: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance

### Retention Criteria
- **Legal Requirements**: Data retained as required by applicable laws
- **Contractual Necessity**: Data retained as necessary for contract performance
- **Legitimate Interests**: Data retained for legitimate business interests
- **Consent Duration**: Data retained only as long as consent is valid

### Data Deletion
- **Automatic Deletion**: Data automatically deleted after retention periods
- **User-Initiated Deletion**: Immediate deletion upon user request
- **Secure Deletion**: Data deleted using secure, non-recoverable methods
- **Deletion Confirmation**: Confirmation provided upon successful deletion

---

## 8. International Data Transfers

### Transfer Mechanisms
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Transfers to countries with EU adequacy decisions
- **Standard Contractual Clauses**: EU-approved contractual protections
- **Binding Corporate Rules**: Internal rules for international transfers
- **Certification Schemes**: Approved certification schemes for data protection

### Transfer Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All international transfers use end-to-end encryption
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls for transferred data
- **Audit Rights**: Regular audits of international transfer safeguards
- **Breach Notification**: Immediate notification of any transfer breaches

### Third Countries
- **Risk Assessment**: Regular risk assessments for third-country transfers
- **Supplemental Measures**: Additional safeguards for high-risk transfers
- **Data Subject Rights**: Protection of data subject rights in international transfers
- **Legal Remedies**: Access to legal remedies for international data issues

## 9. Sub-Processing

### Authorized Sub-Processors
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe, PayPal for secure payment processing
- **Cloud Providers**: AWS, Google Cloud for secure data storage
- **Analytics Providers**: Privacy-compliant analytics services
- **Security Providers**: Security monitoring and threat detection services

### Sub-Processor Requirements
- **Contractual Obligations**: Sub-processors bound by same data protection obligations
- **Security Standards**: Sub-processors must meet equivalent security standards
- **Audit Rights**: GOAT maintains audit rights over sub-processors
- **Change Notification**: Users notified of sub-processor changes

### Sub-Processor Changes
- **Prior Notice**: 30 days notice before adding new sub-processors
- **Objection Rights**: Users can object to new sub-processors
- **Transition Period**: Reasonable transition period for sub-processor changes
- **Data Protection**: No reduction in data protection standards

## 10. Breach Notification

### Breach Response
- **Immediate Response**: Breach investigation begins immediately upon discovery
- **Risk Assessment**: Assessment of risks to data subjects' rights and freedoms
- **Notification Timeline**: Notification to supervisory authorities within 72 hours
- **Data Subject Notification**: Notification to affected data subjects without delay

### Breach Information
- **Breach Details**: Nature, scope, and consequences of the breach
- **Mitigation Measures**: Measures taken to mitigate potential adverse effects
- **Contact Information**: Contact details for further information
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Compliance with all applicable breach notification requirements

## 12. Audit and Inspection

### Audit Rights
- **Annual Audits**: GOAT undergoes annual data protection audits
- **User Audits**: Users can request audits of GOAT's data processing practices
- **Regulatory Audits**: Cooperation with regulatory authority audits
- **Independent Audits**: Audits conducted by independent qualified auditors

- **Process Compliance**: Audit of compliance with data processing procedures

## 13. Termination and Return/Destruction

### Termination Obligations
- **Data Return**: Return or destruction of all personal data upon termination
- **Secure Destruction**: Secure destruction using industry-standard methods
- **Verification**: Verification of complete data destruction
- **Certification**: Certification of data destruction provided to users

### Post-Termination
- **No Further Processing**: Cessation of all data processing activities
- **Data Isolation**: Isolation of data during termination period
- **Access Prevention**: Prevention of further access to personal data
- **Record Retention**: Retention of necessary records for compliance purposes

- **Regulatory Compliance**: Users responsible for overall regulatory compliance
